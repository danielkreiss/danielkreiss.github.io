## Regulatory Frameworks & Alignment

- NIST SP 800-171
- CMMC Level 2

I work closely with security leadership, compliance stakeholders, and executive teams to ensure regulatory requirements are interpreted correctly and implemented in ways that withstand audit scrutiny.

---

## Control Implementation & Enforcement

My approach emphasizes **practical enforcement**, not checkbox compliance:

- Identity and access control aligned to least-privilege principles
- Certificate-based authentication and RADIUS enforcement
- Device compliance and conditional access governance
- Network segmentation and boundary protection
- Centralized logging, monitoring, and evidence collection

Controls are designed to be enforceable, repeatable, and operationally sustainable.

---

## Audit Readiness & Evidence Management

I lead audit preparation by ensuring:

- Policies and procedures reflect actual technical enforcement
- Systems are hardened consistently and verifiably
- Evidence can be produced without operational disruption
- Findings are addressed through sustainable remediation plans

This reduces audit risk while strengthening overall system resilience.

---

## Incident Response & Risk Management

Security incidents are treated as leadership moments. I have led recovery and remediation efforts following ransomware events, balancing technical response, operational continuity, and executive communication.

Risk decisions are deliberate, documented, and aligned with business priorities.

---

## Governance Philosophy

Effective compliance programs succeed when:
- Controls have clear ownership
- Enforcement is automated where possible
- Exceptions are rare, documented, and reviewed
- Security enables operations instead of blocking them

This philosophy supports secure, compliant, and agile organizations.
